Characterizes
Title
Description "This method invokes the .equals(Object o) method on an array. Since arrays do not override the equals method of Object, calling equals on an array is the same as comparing their addresses. To compare the contents of the arrays, use java.util.Arrays.equals(Object[], Object[]).
To compare the addresses of the arrays, it would be less confusing to explicitly pointer equality using ==." [1]
